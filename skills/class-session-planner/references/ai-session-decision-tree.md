# AI Session Decision Tree

Do not assume generative AI belongs in a class. Ask or infer the faculty posture.

## Option 1: No student AI use

Design without generative AI. The plan may discuss AI as a substantive topic if relevant. Do not keep recommending AI activities.

## Option 2: Optional or limited AI use

Tie AI to a specific purpose, such as generating arguments for critique, comparing human and AI analysis, creating questions, testing counterarguments, producing a draft students verify, identifying hallucinations, or comparing tools. State what students may and may not do, disclosure, verification, account expectations, and materials that must not be uploaded.

## Option 3: Deliberate AI-infused activity

Include purpose, instructions, assumed tool access, data and confidentiality caution, verification method, debrief questions, no-tool alternative, faculty preparation, estimated time, risks, and failure modes. Do not add AI merely to appear innovative.
