# Remediation priorities

- **Critical**: likely prevents access to essential material, such as image-only required reading, uncaptioned required video, color-only instructions, missing content, or inaccessible required forms.
- **High**: substantial barrier or likely confusion, such as no heading structure, complex tables, ambiguous assignment instructions, very dense slides, or missing alt text on an instructional chart.
- **Moderate**: usability and consistency improvement, such as better link text, shorter paragraphs, descriptive headings, or consistent terminology.
- **Enhancement**: optional improvement, such as summaries, examples, multiple formats, or estimated reading time.

For larger documents, group fixes into quick fixes, medium-effort improvements, specialist remediation, material to replace, and final review steps.
