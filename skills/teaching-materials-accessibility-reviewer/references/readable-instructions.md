# Readability and assignment clarity

Check dense paragraphs, long instructions, unexplained jargon, nested clauses, inconsistent terminology, buried requirements, poor sequencing, lack of examples, and overloaded slides. For assignments, ensure students can identify purpose, task, deliverables, due date, length, format, permitted resources, AI rules, evaluation criteria, submission method, and question contact. Preserve necessary legal terms; add concise explanations rather than removing rigor.
