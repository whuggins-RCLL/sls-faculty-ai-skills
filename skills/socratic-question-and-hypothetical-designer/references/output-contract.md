# Output contract

Produce only requested components unless the faculty asks for a full package.

Available deliverables: teaching objective map, Socratic question sequence, cold-call pathway, hypothetical packet, factual variation ladder, likely student response guide, discussion timing plan, student preparation guide, faculty teaching notes, and design review.

For each question include a short faculty-only purpose note when giving a sequence. For targeted requests, keep the output targeted and explain what assumptions were made. Separate student-facing and faculty-only material.
