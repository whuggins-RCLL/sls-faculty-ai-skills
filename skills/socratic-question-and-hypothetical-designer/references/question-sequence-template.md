# Socratic question sequence template

## Teaching objective map
| Objective | Question or hypothetical | Evidence of learning | Difficulty |
|---|---|---|---|

## Opening questions
List question, purpose tag, difficulty, faculty-only purpose note, expected answer range, and recovery option.

## Core sequence
Progress through fact selection, rule identification, reasoning, application, counterargument, policy, comparison, and professional judgment as relevant.

## Branching prompts
Use the branch format in `branching-follow-ups.md` for major questions.

## Variations and closing
Include factual variations, synthesis, and a closing question that identifies what students should carry forward.
