# Hypothetical packet template

## Student-facing facts
Clearly separate facts from teaching notes. If fictional, begin with: FICTIONAL INSTRUCTIONAL HYPOTHETICAL — not a real case or authority.

## Question presented
State the legal or professional judgment issue.

## Student task
Specify discussion, writing, argument, drafting, role play, or assessment use. Include time and optional roles.

## Faculty-only teaching notes
Include design note, expected issues, arguments on each side, factual variants, debrief questions, sensitive-topic review, and verification notes.
