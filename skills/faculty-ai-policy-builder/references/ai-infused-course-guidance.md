# AI-infused course guidance

AI-infused policies intentionally incorporate AI into learning through critiquing generated analysis, verifying hallucinated citations, comparing outputs, revising flawed drafts, auditing bias, documenting prompt iterations, or reflecting on professional judgment.

Define required AI use, optional use, no-AI work, access alternatives, verification, reflection deliverables, documentation, confidentiality rules, and grading criteria. Do not impose AI use if the learning purpose does not call for it.
