# AI Policy Template

## Purpose
Explain why this AI-use rule supports the course or assignment learning goals.

## General Rule
State the selected posture: AI-restricted, AI-limited, AI-flexible with disclosure, or AI-infused.

## Permitted Uses
List specific activities, stages, and conditions.

## Prohibited Uses
List activities, materials, or stages where AI may not be used.

## Uses Requiring Permission
Identify uncertain or higher-risk uses students should ask about first.

## Verification Requirements
Explain what students must independently check, especially for authorities, facts, quotations, and citations.

## Disclosure Requirements
Explain when disclosure is required and what information students must provide.

## Confidentiality and Data Restrictions
List materials that may not be uploaded to AI tools.

## Assignment-Specific Variation
Explain whether assignment notices override the course rule.

## Examples
Include policy-specific examples of permitted, prohibited, and permission-required uses.
