# Legal Authority Verification

For cases record full case name, court, year, citation when verified, relevant proposition, assigned excerpt or full opinion, current-status caution, and relationship to other assigned cases. For statutes, regulations, and rules record jurisdiction, issuing body, date or version, section, and official source. Do not fabricate citations or claim good-law status without citator-level verification. Faculty-provided but unverified authorities must be labeled as faculty-provided and not independently verified.
