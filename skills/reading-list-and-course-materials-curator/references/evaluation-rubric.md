# Evaluation Rubric

Before finalizing, confirm: every required item has a title; every recommended item has a source type; every item has instructional purpose, access status, and verification status; required and optional items are distinguishable; verified items have verification dates; workload totals are present; tiers are consistent; faculty-only notes are separated; legal authorities include current-status cautions; no source, citation, page range, quote, access claim, or good-law status is invented.
