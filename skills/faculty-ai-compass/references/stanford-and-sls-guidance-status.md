# Stanford and SLS guidance status

Use live web research when routing depends on:

- Current Stanford AI services
- Current tool availability
- Current data classification
- Current privacy or security guidance
- Current teaching or research guidance
- Current accessibility guidance
- Current institutional processes
- Current product tiers or terms

## Source hierarchy

Distinguish clearly:

- SLS-approved guidance
- Stanford University policy or standard
- Stanford University guidance
- Stanford service information
- SLS Faculty AI Lab instructional principle
- Vendor claim
- Independent evidence
- No public source located
- Local confirmation required
