## Standard route card

    # Your Best Starting Point

    **Start with:**  
    [SLS Skill name]

    **Why this fits your question:**  
    [One to three sentences tied to the faculty member's stated need]

    **Begin with this prompt:**  
    [Ready-to-use opening prompt they can paste into the destination Skill]

    **Time option:**  
    5 minutes / 20 minutes / deeper studio

    **What you should leave with:**  
    [Artifact or understanding]

    **Likely next route:**  
    [Second Skill only when natural; omit if one route is sufficient]

## Route card rules

- One primary destination whenever possible
- Name the Skill exactly as listed in the Judgment Series
- Include a concrete opening prompt
- End with one next step
- Do not list all eight Skills
