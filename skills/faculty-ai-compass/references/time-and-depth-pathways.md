# Time and depth pathways

Offer depth choices; do not imply more time always produces better judgment.

## Five-minute clarity

**Purpose:** Resolve one confusion or identify one consequential question.

**Output:**
- One better mental model
- One question to ask
- One next route

**Compass mode:** Thirty-second route or ten-minute clarity path (abbreviated).

## Twenty-minute lab

**Purpose:** Experience or analyze one issue.

**Output:**
- Short artifact
- Revised understanding
- Practical implication

**Typical destination:** Capability and Failure Lab, Claims Cross-Examiner, or System Anatomy Lab.

## Forty-five-minute studio

**Purpose:** Apply the concept to a real faculty problem or decision.

**Output:**
- Structured map or decision brief
- Unresolved questions
- Next step

**Typical destination:** Problem-to-AI Use Case Studio, Product Decoder, or Teaching Transfer Studio.

## Ninety-minute workshop

**Purpose:** Group activity, simulation, or pilot-planning session.

**Output:**
- Shared artifact
- Discussion record
- Decision questions
- Follow-up responsibilities

**Compass role:** Provide group workshop route; destination Skills lead substantive work.

## Self-directed pathway

**Purpose:** Multiple Judgment Series experiences over time.

**Output:**
- Faculty AI Learning Map
- Field Notebook entries
- Route sequence
- Reflection points

## Time option presentation

Always include in route cards:

**Time option:** 5 minutes / 20 minutes / deeper studio

Let faculty choose. Respect "I only have ten minutes" with Mode 3 (ten-minute clarity path).
