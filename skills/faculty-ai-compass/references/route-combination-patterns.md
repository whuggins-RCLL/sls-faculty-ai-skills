# Route combination patterns

Use combinations only when they serve the faculty member's purpose. Do not force sequences when one Skill is sufficient.

## Understand → test

1. SLS AI System Anatomy Lab
2. SLS AI Capability and Failure Lab

**When:** Faculty need the mental model before a direct experiment.

## Decode → challenge → test

1. SLS AI Product Decoder
2. SLS AI Claims Cross-Examiner
3. SLS AI Capability and Failure Lab

**When:** A current product and its claims require scrutiny.

## Problem → pilot → govern

1. SLS Problem-to-AI Use Case Studio
2. SLS AI Pilot and Evaluation Designer
3. SLS AI Governance and Human Judgment Simulator

**When:** An idea may become an institutional workflow.

## Experience → teach

1. SLS AI Capability and Failure Lab
2. SLS AI Teaching Transfer Studio

**When:** Faculty want to turn a failure mode into student learning.

## Understand → govern

1. SLS AI System Anatomy Lab
2. SLS AI Governance and Human Judgment Simulator

**When:** Agents, connectors, or institutional control are confusing.

## Claim → teaching

1. SLS AI Claims Cross-Examiner
2. SLS AI Teaching Transfer Studio

**When:** Students should evaluate a prominent AI claim.

## Product → problem reset

1. SLS AI Product Decoder
2. SLS Problem-to-AI Use Case Studio

**When:** A product has been proposed before the problem is defined.

## Suggested pathway cards (collection level)

### I feel lost

Compass → System Anatomy Lab → Capability and Failure Lab

### I heard a product claim

Compass → Product Decoder → Claims Cross-Examiner

### I have a problem to solve

Compass → Problem-to-AI Use Case Studio → Pilot Designer

### Institutional agent or workflow

Compass → System Anatomy Lab → Governance Simulator → Pilot Designer

### Teach students about AI

Compass → Capability and Failure Lab → Teaching Transfer Studio

### Vendor demonstration prep

Compass → Product Decoder
