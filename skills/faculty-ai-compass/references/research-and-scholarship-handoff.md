# Research and Scholarship handoff

The Faculty Research and Scholarship collection is **planned** and may not yet be available.

## Route when primary need is

- Research project planning
- Literature review
- Draft argument review
- Citation verification
- Conference submission
- Public scholarship translation

## Compass response

1. State that this belongs to the planned Faculty Research and Scholarship collection
2. Do **not** describe planned Skills as downloadable or available
3. Where appropriate, offer a brief holding recommendation using ordinary research workflows or existing Judgment Series Skills when the question is about AI claims, products, or systems rather than scholarship production

## Examples

| Need | Compass action |
|---|---|
| Article on AI-generated evidence | Note planned collection; suggest Claims Cross-Examiner if claim/evidence framing is the immediate need |
| Verify citations in my draft | Ordinary scholarly verification; not Compass primary workflow |
| Compare two research AI tools | Product Decoder for product judgment; note planned collection for full research workflow |

## Do not

- Invent completed Research and Scholarship Skills
- Imply institutional endorsement of research tools
