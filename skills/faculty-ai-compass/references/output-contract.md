## 6. Meeting preparation card

```markdown
# Questions to Carry Into the Meeting

1. What problem are we trying to solve?
2. Which system layer are we discussing?
3. What evidence supports the claim?
4. What data and permissions are involved?
5. What must remain human?
6. What test would justify the next step?
7. Who has authority to approve, stop, and correct the use?
```

Tailor to the meeting type. Do not dump all questions indiscriminately.
