# Teaching and Course Design handoffs

When the faculty member needs a **complete teaching deliverable**, route from Teaching Transfer Studio context to the appropriate Teaching and Course Design Skill.

## Handoff table

| Faculty need | Destination Skill |
|---|---|
| Entire course or syllabus | Course and Syllabus Designer |
| One class session | Class Session Planner |
| Assignment, exam, or rubric | Assessment and Rubric Builder |
| Course or assignment AI rules | Faculty AI Policy Builder |
| Questions or hypotheticals | Socratic Question and Hypothetical Designer |
| Accessibility audit | Teaching Materials Accessibility Reviewer |
| Reading and course materials | Reading List and Course Materials Curator |

## Routing logic

| Prompt pattern | Primary route | Handoff |
|---|---|---|
| Should students use AI for this assignment? | Teaching Transfer Studio | Policy Builder, Assessment Builder as needed |
| Redesign my entire course around AI | Course and Syllabus Designer | Teaching Transfer Studio may precede for judgment framing |
| I need rules for student AI use | Faculty AI Policy Builder | No forced Compass diagnostic |
| Is this AI activity accessible? | Teaching Transfer Studio for activity design; Accessibility Reviewer for full audit | Explain distinction |
| Readings about AI and legal practice | Reading List and Course Materials Curator | Not Product Decoder unless products are the subject |

## Handoff format

Provide:

- Destination name
- Why it fits
- Ready-to-use opening prompt
- Expected artifact
- Likely follow-up

Do not duplicate the destination workflow.

## Collection relationship

The SLS AI Judgment Series builds judgment about AI systems and evidence. The Teaching and Course Design collection produces course artifacts. The Compass explains the relationship without merging workflows.
