# Misconception and mental model signals

## Reset pattern

1. Reflect the reasonable concern or partial truth.
2. Identify the misleading part.
3. Provide a better mental model.
4. Recommend the relevant route.
5. Avoid embarrassing language.

## Common misconceptions and routes

### Uploading documents trains the model

**Partial truth:** Upload may provide material for the current task or retrieval.  
**Misleading:** Not automatically the same as training the underlying model.  
**Route:** SLS AI System Anatomy Lab.

### Citations mean the answer is verified

**Partial truth:** Citations can point to sources.  
**Misleading:** Models can cite incorrectly, cite irrelevant sources, or omit key authority.  
**Route:** SLS AI Claims Cross-Examiner or Capability and Failure Lab.

### A successful demo proves readiness

**Partial truth:** Demos show one possible output.  
**Misleading:** One success does not establish reliability under representative conditions.  
**Route:** SLS AI Pilot and Evaluation Designer or Capability and Failure Lab.

### Human review of every output is sufficient

**Partial truth:** Human review can catch errors.  
**Misleading:** Review must be meaningful, timely, and supported by workflow design.  
**Route:** SLS AI Governance and Human Judgment Simulator.

### AI everywhere in a course improves learning

**Partial truth:** AI can support some learning tasks.  
**Misleading:** Tool-first adoption can undermine objectives and assessment validity.  
**Route:** SLS AI Teaching Transfer Studio.

### Open-source or local means private and approved

**Partial truth:** Deployment choices affect privacy and control.  
**Misleading:** License, hosting, logging, and institutional approval are separate questions.  
**Route:** SLS AI System Anatomy Lab; Governance Simulator if institutional use.

### Retrieval eliminates hallucinations

**Partial truth:** Retrieval can ground answers in selected material.  
**Misleading:** Retrieval does not guarantee completeness, correct interpretation, or faithful citation.  
**Route:** SLS AI Capability and Failure Lab.
