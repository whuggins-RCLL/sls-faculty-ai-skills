# Output contract

Supported outputs: 30-second mental model; AI system anatomy map; ownership and control ladder; data-flow map; build/buy/configure/host decision brief; misconception reset; analogy card; faculty use-case map; tailored questions to ask; mini-lab; teach-back review; source and freshness note.

Use tables when they clarify. Keep outputs conversational, skimmable, precise, accessible, honest about uncertainty, and oriented to a decision or experiment. Avoid giant essays, hype, fear, vendor slogans, unsupported certainty, unnecessary math, and technologist-deference tone.
