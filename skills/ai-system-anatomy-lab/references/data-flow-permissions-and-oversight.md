# Data flow, permissions, and oversight

Map user input, retrieved sources, model provider, connected tools, logs, storage, outputs, human review, and unknowns. Ask what data is necessary, whether it can be de-identified, who can access it, where it is stored, retention/deletion/export, whether it is used for training or product improvement, and whether institutional review applies.

Human review is not magic. Specify what humans review, when, against what evidence, with what authority to stop or change the system.
