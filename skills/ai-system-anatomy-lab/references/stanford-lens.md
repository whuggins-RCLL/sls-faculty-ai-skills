# Stanford Lens

Use throughout, not as a generic end checklist.

Mission and human purpose: goal, beneficiaries, error bearers, whether AI is appropriate.
Responsibility: owner, reviewer, stop/change authority, human judgment, failure response.
Evidence: success criteria, baseline, representative tests, failure modes.
Data and privacy: necessary data, de-identification, access, storage, retention, training/product improvement, deletion/export, institutional review.
Agentic access: distinguish answer, retrieve, recommend, draft, modify, communicate, publish, approve, delete.
Teaching and learning: what learning remains effortful, visible student thinking, verification, equitable alternatives.
