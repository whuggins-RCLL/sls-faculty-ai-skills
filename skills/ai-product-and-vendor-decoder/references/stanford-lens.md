# Stanford Lens

Mission: What faculty problem would this solve? Is AI necessary? Who benefits and who bears errors?

Responsibility: Who owns, reviews, approves, and remains human in the loop? What happens on failure?

Evidence: What baseline exists? What representative testing exists? Were failures and omissions measured? Is evidence vendor-produced?

Data: What enters the product? Is it necessary and de-identifiable? Who receives it? Where stored, how long retained, used for training/improvement, deletable/exportable?

Access and action: distinguish can answer, retrieve, recommend, draft, modify, send, publish, approve, delete.

Teaching and research: what faculty work it supports, what should remain effortful, source verification, materials not to upload, equal access, and no-tool alternative.
