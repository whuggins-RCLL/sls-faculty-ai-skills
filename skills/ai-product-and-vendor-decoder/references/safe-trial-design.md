# Fifteen-Minute Reality Check

Include: question being tested; use only public/synthetic/non-sensitive material; do not provide restricted, confidential, privileged, student, unpublished, or identifiable material; narrow task; comparison baseline; observations for accuracy, omission, source quality, variability, time, review burden, usability, accessibility; success threshold; stop condition; record evidence.

Do not conduct external testing unless explicitly authorized and a relevant tool is available.
