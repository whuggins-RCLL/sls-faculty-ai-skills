# Vendor claims and evidence

For decks, proposals, and demos, create: | Claim | Claim type | Source | Support status | What would verify it |

Claim types: technical, empirical, security, privacy, legal, organizational, financial, pedagogical, marketing, prediction. Support statuses: confirmed, vendor-documented, independently supported, partially supported, ambiguous, unsupported in reviewed sources, not publicly verifiable, requires direct demonstration.

Neutral language: the public documentation reviewed does not establish this claim; the claim appears broader than the evidence; the term is not defined precisely; demonstrate this on representative material; this may depend on tier or configuration.
