# Architecture X-Ray

Use this stack when helpful:

Faculty member
      ↓
Product interface
      ↓
Vendor workflow and instructions
      ↓
Retrieval or knowledge layer
      ↓
Tools, connectors, and permissions
      ↓
Underlying model provider or providers
      ↓
External services, storage, and logging

For each layer use: | Layer | Confirmed provider | Function | Data handled | Evidence | Unknown |

Identify where prompts and uploads go, which organization controls each layer, models used, model-change rights, storage, logging, product-improvement/training uses, subprocessors, admin controls, and unknowns. Do not infer architecture from branding.
