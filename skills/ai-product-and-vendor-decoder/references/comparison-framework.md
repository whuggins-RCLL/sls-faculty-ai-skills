# Comparison framework

Compare against: underlying base model or general assistant; direct competitors; Stanford-provided or listed option when verified; simpler configured workflow; non-AI alternative; current process baseline.

Use: | Dimension | Product | Alternative | Why it matters |

Dimensions may include problem fit, source grounding, citation provenance, model transparency, data use, retention, permissions, agent actions, controls, evaluation evidence, accessibility, exportability, integration, review burden, cost, dependency, and exit strategy. Do not pick a winner without explicit criteria, evidence, weights, unknowns, and date checked.
