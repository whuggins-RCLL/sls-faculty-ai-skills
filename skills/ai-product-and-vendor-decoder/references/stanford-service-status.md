# Stanford service status

Always search current official Stanford sources for Stanford claims. Record one status: public Stanford service page located; guidance located; data-classification information located; listing located but exact tier differs; no relevant public listing located; ambiguous; requires process confirmation.

Never say absence from public pages means “Stanford has not approved this product.” Use: “I did not locate this product in the public Stanford materials checked on [date]. That does not establish whether it has or has not been approved for a particular use. Confirm the intended use, data type, account tier, and current Stanford review requirements.”
