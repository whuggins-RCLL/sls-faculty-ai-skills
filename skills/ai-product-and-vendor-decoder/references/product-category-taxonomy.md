# Product category taxonomy

Use one or more categories: foundation-model provider; model or model family; model hosting platform; general-purpose assistant; enterprise AI platform; search or answer engine; retrieval or knowledge system; agent or orchestration platform; workflow automation layer; domain-specific application; legal-technology application; education application; research application; content-generation product; coding product; meeting or transcription product; interface layer; connector or integration layer; evaluation or observability product; security or governance product; hybrid.

Explain why category matters for evidence, data flow, comparators, review burden, and alternatives. Do not force hybrid products into one label.
