# Output contract

## The 60-Second Take
Fields: What it is; What it is not; Product category; What appears to power it; Closest meaningful comparisons; Stanford status; Strongest plausible faculty use; Main concern; Bottom line; Research checked.

## Full report
Include the 60-Second Take first; exact identity; category; Architecture X-Ray; dependencies; data-flow map; differentiation; Stanford Lens/status; comparisons; faculty use cases; poor fits; demo questions; Fifteen-Minute Reality Check; unknowns; sources/freshness; bottom-line next step.

## Comparison report
Faculty problem, products/versions, shared category, differences, dependencies, source grounding, data handling, controls, evidence, use cases, cost when verified, unknowns, safe test, no-AI alternative. No winner without criteria.

## Wrapper report
Meaning of term, dependencies, added workflow/knowledge/governance/evaluation value, replicability, lock-in, evidence, confidence, questions.
