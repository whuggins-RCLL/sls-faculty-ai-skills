# Product identity and versioning

Before evaluation, record: research date, exact product, vendor, feature, tier, version/release channel, availability, intended customer, geography, account limits, and whether the item is a model, application, platform, or feature.

Do not combine capabilities from different tiers, accounts, regions, products, or release channels. If the tier/version is not public, say so and treat claims as conditional.

Ambiguity examples: Copilot may mean Microsoft 365 Copilot, GitHub Copilot, Windows Copilot, Security Copilot, or another product. Ask before merging facts.
