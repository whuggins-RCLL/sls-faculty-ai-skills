# Fictional scenario safeguards

## Do not attribute scenarios to

- The Dean
- Named SLS leaders
- Named faculty
- Current committees
- Recognizable internal proposals
- Real students

Use fictional composite scenarios.

## Required label for composites

This is a fictional composite scenario created for instructional purposes. It is not based on or attributed to an identified Stanford individual or proposal.

## Fictional legal authority label

**FICTIONAL INSTRUCTIONAL MATERIAL — not real legal authority**

Use obviously instructional names. Never create real-looking fictional citations that could be mistaken for actual authority outside the exercise.

## Real internal scenarios from faculty

- Analyze only supplied facts
- Do not infer motives
- Do not embellish
- Do not search confidential details publicly
- Do not recreate the situation as a fictional story about the person

## Vendor and product demos

Use public or synthetic inputs. Do not use confidential institutional data to make demos realistic.
