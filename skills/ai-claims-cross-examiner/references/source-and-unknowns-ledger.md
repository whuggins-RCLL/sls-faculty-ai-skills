# Source and unknowns ledger

Every full report should include a ledger:

| Question or proposition | Status | Evidence | Date | Confidence | Next step |

Status examples: confirmed; partly supported; disputed; unknown; requires verification; definition-dependent; not publicly verifiable.

## What I Could Not Verify

List gaps explicitly. Do not hide uncertainty in footnotes.

## Confidence dimensions

Separate when relevant:

- Confidence that the source says what it says
- Confidence in the evidence
- Confidence that evidence applies to faculty context
- Confidence in any prediction

## Full examination confidence block

Confidence in the provisional finding: High / Moderate / Low

Reason: evidence quality, agreement, currency, scope, important gaps.

## Research freshness

Always include research checked date when current evidence was reviewed.
