# Faculty implications

Every full claim examination should conclude with relevant categories from:

## Teaching

- Is the claim safe to repeat as fact?
- What qualification should accompany it?
- Could students test it?
- What misconception should be avoided?

## Research

- Is the claim a research lead, established fact, or unresolved question?
- What primary source should be read?
- What evidence should not be cited without qualification?

## Product evaluation

- Which claim should be tested during a demo?
- What evidence should be requested?
- Does the claim belong in the Product Decoder workflow?

## Institutional decision

- Is evidence sufficient for exploration?
- Is a narrow pilot appropriate?
- Does the claim require privacy, security, legal, or policy confirmation?
- What decision should not yet be made?

## Public communication

- What wording is accurate?
- What wording would overstate the evidence?
- What caveat is essential?

Choose only relevant categories. Do not force all sections when irrelevant.
