# Output contract

Generate only requested deliverables. Do not produce every output unless asked.

## Output 1: The 30-Second Read

# The 30-Second Read

**Claim:** exact wording

**Provisional finding:** calibrated label

**Why:** two or three sentences

**Most important limitation:** one item

**What to take away:** practical meaning

**Research checked:** date when applicable

## Output 2: Full Claim Examination

Include: exact claim; source and date; claim type; important definitions; scope; strongest defensible version; evidence required; evidence found; counterevidence; alternative explanations; what the claim does not establish; provisional finding; confidence; faculty implications; smallest useful test; what could not be verified; sources and freshness.

## Output 3: Claim Anatomy Map

Compact table per claim-anatomy.md.

## Output 4: Evidence Audit

Review one study, report, benchmark, or graph with method, limits, headline comparison, and applicability.

Evaluate: accurate; overstated; missing qualification; misleading analogy; strongest next revision.

## Required elements for full examination

Exact claim; claim type; evidence status; confidence; unknowns; research date when current evidence used; no unsupported final verdict; no prohibited binary label without supporting rationale.
