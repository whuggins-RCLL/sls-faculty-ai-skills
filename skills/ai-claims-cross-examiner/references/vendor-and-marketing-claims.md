# Vendor and marketing claims

For vendor claims:

1. Preserve exact wording.
2. Identify undefined adjectives (enterprise-grade, proprietary, hallucination-free).
3. Search vendor official support and documentation.
4. Search for independent evidence.
5. Distinguish capability from outcome.
6. Identify product tier or configuration.
7. Identify whether claim is demonstrated or measured.
8. Ask what failures were excluded.
9. Note conflicts of interest.
10. Do not accuse dishonesty without evidence.

## Vendor-claim check table

| Claim | Defined? | Vendor support | Independent support | Unknown | Demo test |

## Finding labels

- Supported by current vendor documentation
- Independently supported
- Partially supported
- Broader than the reviewed evidence
- Undefined
- Not publicly verifiable
- Requires a representative demonstration
- Requires contractual or institutional confirmation

## Marketing versus measurement

“Proprietary AI” may mean proprietary model, routing, retrieval, data, prompts, interface, or workflow. Separate each layer and ask for evidence.
