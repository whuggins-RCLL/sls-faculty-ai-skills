# Restricted Materials

Do not include restricted, confidential, or personally identifiable material in AI workflows unless the institution has explicitly approved the workflow and tool environment.

## Examples of restricted material

- Student education records, grades, accommodations, advising notes, or private communications.
- Identifiable student work without documented permission and approved handling.
- Unpublished exams, answer keys, secure assessment banks, or proctoring materials.
- Confidential institutional data, contracts, personnel records, or legal advice.
- Licensed third-party content whose terms prohibit AI processing or redistribution.

## Safer alternatives

- Use synthetic examples.
- Redact identifying details.
- Summarize constraints without exposing protected text.
- Link to approved internal sources rather than copying restricted content.
- Ask an authorized institutional reviewer before proceeding when classification is unclear.
