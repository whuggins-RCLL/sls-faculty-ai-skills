# Institutional Policy Placeholders

Replace these placeholders with locally approved language before using generated materials with learners.

## Common placeholders

- `[INSTITUTION_NAME]`
- `[ACCESSIBILITY_OFFICE_STATEMENT]`
- `[ACADEMIC_INTEGRITY_POLICY]`
- `[AI_USE_POLICY]`
- `[ATTENDANCE_POLICY]`
- `[GRADING_SCALE_OR_SCHEMA]`
- `[LATE_WORK_POLICY]`
- `[TITLE_IX_OR_EQUITY_REPORTING_STATEMENT]`
- `[STUDENT_SUPPORT_RESOURCES]`
- `[DATA_PRIVACY_OR_LEARNING_TECHNOLOGY_NOTICE]`

## Use guidance

- Do not fabricate official policy language.
- Keep required policy text verbatim when the institution requires exact language.
- Include the approval date or source location for inserted policy language when available.
- Escalate unresolved policy questions to an authorized institutional reviewer.
