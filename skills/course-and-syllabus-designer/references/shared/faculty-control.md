# Faculty Control

AI skills in this repository must preserve faculty authority over instructional design.

## Required practices

- Treat AI output as draft support, not final academic judgment.
- Ask faculty to confirm learning goals, instructional constraints, grading policies, and assessment choices.
- Flag choices that require faculty decision rather than silently resolving them.
- Preserve the instructor's voice, course context, and disciplinary expectations.
- Make assumptions explicit when source material is incomplete.

## Do not

- Invent binding course policies without faculty approval.
- Replace instructor-selected readings, assessments, or grading schemes without confirmation.
- Present generated content as institutionally approved unless an approved source says so.
