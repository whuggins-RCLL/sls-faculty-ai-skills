# Reading List Template

Provide a separate reading list even if readings also appear in the syllabus. For each item include available metadata: author, title, publication, year, citation, URL/database, excerpt/pages, required/recommended/optional status, session/unit, purpose, verification status, accessibility/availability concern, and permission/copyright concern requiring review.

Separate confirmed assigned materials, recommended additions, alternatives, faculty-review items, and materials that could not be verified or accessed.
