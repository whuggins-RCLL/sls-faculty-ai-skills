# Assessment Alignment

Align assessments with objectives, class activities, and feedback opportunities. Flag assessment misalignment, unrealistic workload, overloaded sessions, missing formative feedback, unclear expectations, grading ambiguities, and participation policies that may disadvantage students without alternatives.

When drafting grading structures, use placeholders where faculty or institutional policy must decide weights, curves, anonymity, late work, attendance, accommodations, or academic-integrity consequences.
