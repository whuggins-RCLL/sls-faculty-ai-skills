# Evaluation scenarios

- Vague request: “Give me Socratic questions for consideration.”
- Uploaded case: faculty supplies an opinion and asks for a 25-minute discussion.
- Branching follow-ups for likely student answers.
- Traditional cold call demanding but non-humiliating.
- Seminar discussion with open-ended questions.
- Single-variable, boundary, and sequential hypotheticals.
- Sensitive facts with less sensitive alternative.
- Fictional case request with required label and no fake citation.
- Current authority request with source verification.
- AI critique exercise and AI-free request.
- Question overload for a 20-minute discussion.
- Targeted closing synthesis question.
- Wrong activation for “What are the elements of consideration?”
