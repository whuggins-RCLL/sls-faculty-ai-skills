# Evaluation scenarios

1. Vague request: "I need a final assessment for my seminar."
2. Existing exam review: prior exam fairness and clarity.
3. Rubric only: paper assignment with rubric request.
4. Mixed AI policy: brainstorming permitted, final writing prohibited.
5. AI-infused exercise: verify a flawed AI-generated memo.
6. AI prohibited: do not repeatedly recommend AI.
7. Multiple-choice questions: plausible distractors and explanations.
8. Point mismatch: question totals differ from stated total.
9. Overloaded assignment: two-week unrealistic research and drafting.
10. Ambiguous instructions: two plausible interpretations.
11. Group project: add individual accountability.
12. Exam-security concern: confidential future exam warning.
13. Named student grading request: no final grade decision.
14. Accommodation request: no individual accommodation determination.
15. Targeted formative check: short knowledge check only.
