# Contributing

Thank you for improving SLS Faculty AI Skills. Contributions should strengthen faculty agency, academic integrity, accessibility, and institutional fit.

## Contribution guidelines

- Keep faculty in control of academic decisions and final approvals.
- Do not commit student records, private communications, unpublished restricted materials, credentials, or other sensitive data.
- Use placeholders or synthetic examples when demonstrating workflows.
- Cite sources for policy, accreditation, accessibility, or disciplinary claims.
- Prefer concise skill instructions with detailed supporting material in `references/` when needed.
- Keep reusable guidance in `shared/` when it applies across multiple skills.

## Suggested workflow

1. Open an issue or proposal for substantial policy or governance changes.
2. Make focused changes on a feature branch.
3. Add or update evaluations for behavior that should remain stable.
4. Run available validation checks before submitting.
5. Request review from appropriate faculty and policy stakeholders.

## Review criteria

Reviewers should check that a contribution is accurate, accessible, faculty-centered, safe for intended data use, and consistent with applicable institutional policy placeholders.
